Introduction to
Vashisht Dham Sewa Trust

Vashisht Dham Sewa Trust is a non-profit organization dedicated to selfless service across social, spiritual, health and humanitarian domains. Guided by the principle of Seva Parmo Dharma (Service is the highest duty), we aim to uplift the underprivileged and support inclusive community development.

Our mission is to serve elderly and disabled individuals, empower women through skills and self-help groups, promote education in underserved areas, conduct health and environmental campaigns, and foster community spirit through cultural and national celebrations.
