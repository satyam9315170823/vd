Education Initiatives

Empowering Through Education

Vasistha Dham Sewa Trust is committed to nurturing knowledge and values in children through active participation, cultural activities, and educational support programs.

Republic Day 2024 Celebration

VDST volunteers actively participated in Republic Day 2024, instilling patriotism and encouraging young children to honor the values of our nation.

Birthday Celebration with Children

On 1st May 2024, Laxmi Panday celebrated his birthday with children in association with VDST, spreading love and joy among young hearts.

Stationary & Food Distribution

VDST distributed stationary and nutritious food to BPL children at Chhatarpur, ensuring their educational and health needs are supported.

Ramleela Competition

VDST organized a Ramleela Pratiyogita for BPL children at Chhatarpur, fostering cultural awareness and creativity among young minds.

Biocompost Collector Installation

VDST installed biocompost collectors at GGSSS Vasant Kunj, GGSSS Chhatarpur, Fatehpur Beri, and Govt. Co-ed Senior School Maidan Garhi to promote waste segregation and compost formation.

Story Writing Competition

In collaboration with Vidyanjali, VDST organized a story writing competition at various schools to develop writing skills and help children express their emotions.
